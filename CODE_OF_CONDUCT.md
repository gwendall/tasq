# Tasq community conduct

Be direct, curious and respectful. Critique designs and evidence, not people.
Do not harass, threaten, demean, discriminate against or expose private
information about another participant. Respect a maintainer's decision to
stop unsafe disclosure, credential sharing or publication of private ledger
data.

Project spaces include issues, pull requests, discussions, release channels
and events represented as Tasq community work. Enforcement may include a
private warning, removal of content, temporary restriction or permanent ban,
proportional to severity and recurrence.

Report conduct concerns privately to `gwendall@metahood.xyz`. The maintainer
will minimize disclosure and recuse or appoint an external reviewer if the
maintainer is the subject of a credible report.
